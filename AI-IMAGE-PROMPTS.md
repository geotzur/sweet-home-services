# Sweet Home Services - AI Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts in **Google AI Studio (Gemini 2.0 Flash)** or **Imagen 3** to generate
all the visual assets needed for the website.

---

## 1. Logo Icon (Primary)

**Prompt:**
```
Create a modern, minimalist logo icon for a tech company called "Sweet Home Services".
The icon is a stylized house silhouette with wifi/signal arcs radiating upward from the
roof peak. Use deep teal (#1A6B6B) for the house shape and warm amber (#F5A623) for the
wifi arcs and a small dot at the peak. The design should be flat, geometric, and
vector-style — suitable for use as a favicon and app icon. Pure white background.
No text. Clean edges, no gradients, no shadows. Square 1:1 aspect ratio.
```

**Sizes needed:** 512x512 (master), then resize to 192x192, 180x180 (apple-touch), 32x32 (favicon)
